Your used e-cigs can help Ukrainian defenders!

Bring your used e-cigs

You don't even have to donate money - just bring your used e-cigs or pods from vape, even batteries from old gadgets.

We extract the batteries

The electronic devices are stripped down to their basic elements, and we take out the battery, which we will use further.

Conversion to powerbanks

The energy accumulators in your old devices are valuable units that are very useful in the creation of new energy holders, which are then delivered to the frontlines to our defenders.
